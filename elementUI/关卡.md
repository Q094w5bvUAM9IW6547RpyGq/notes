## table 某个单元显示多个数据 数据格式为数组

```vue
<el-table-column
   prop="productName"
   label="采购最多建材"
   width="100">
   <template slot-scope="scope">
      <div slot="reference" v-for="(item,index) in scope.row.productName">
        <span> {{item}} </span>
      </div>
   </template>
</el-table-column>
```
